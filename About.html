About Us



Hello there! We're Anee and Anna, and we're delighted to meet you. We're the faces behind our YouTube channel, where we share our passion for simple, healthy Indian recipes and the natural lifestyle we embrace on our farmhouse nestled in the dense forests of Odisha, India.


Our journey into organic and natural farming was inspired by our beloved grandparents, Mr. Godfrey and Ms. Bernadine Francis, who instilled in us a deep love for sustainable living. Despite our bustling lives in the heart of Mumbai, we frequently escape to our serene farmhouse to bask in the lush greenery and inner peace it offers.


In addition to our YouTube adventures, we're also dedicated students, always striving to expand our knowledge. We want to express our gratitude and love to all of you who follow our channel.
