Sisters Farm and Kitchen


"Sisters Farm and Kitchen" is a YouTube channel focusing on homemade cooking and farmhouse living. The channel is managed by Aneez and Aneeza Fatima, two sisters from India.


They celebrate Indian cuisine, sharing traditional recipes and infusing them with their unique touch. The channel has a community of 2,500 followers on YouTube. Aneeza Fatima's Facebook profile has amassed an impressive following of 180,000 followers on her Reels content. This achievement reflects the engaging and creative content she shares, which resonates well with her audience.


Their content is a blend of culinary expertise and a love for the rural lifestyle. Viewers can expect to find mouthwatering recipes and a glimpse into farm life. The channel emphasizes farm-to-table cooking and sustainable living. It offers a connection to the beauty of India's diverse culinary traditions.
